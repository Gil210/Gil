LFP Postpones Lens vs PSG

This was published on 29th March 2026 at 7:30pm

The Ligue 1 game between Lens & PSG scheduled for the first week of April has been postponed due to the fact that PSG are still in the Champions League.

PSG play Liverpool next in the Champions League Quarter Finals and they want all players to have the necessary rest and appropriate training required for the important clash with the English Champions.

Lens vs PSG has been postponed to allow the French Champions to focus on their CL tie with Liverpool.

Source: Sky Sports

Italy win 2-0 to reach Playoff Finals

This was published on 29th March 2026

Italy are one step away from the FIFA world cup after defeating Northern Ireland by a 2-0 scoreline this past Thursday in their playoff semifinal pathway.

The match was far from easy for the Italians who were playing for pride knowing that they had not been in the World Cup since 2014. Moise Kean (Fiorentina) broke the deadlock at 24 minutes of the game before Sandro Tonali (Newcastle) added a second goal in the 54th minute to seal the win for the Italians.

Italy now face Bosnia-Herzegovina in the play off final after they defeated Wales on Penalties to reach the final after a 1-1 draw during regular time.

During this game, Bosnia legend Edin Dzeko added more history to his 20 plus years career to be the first Bosnian to score in 20 straight years for his country.

The Italians will be aware of his goal threat despite turning 40 years old earlier in the month. The final takes place this coming Tuesday in Bosnia with the prize for the winning country being a World Cup ticket.
